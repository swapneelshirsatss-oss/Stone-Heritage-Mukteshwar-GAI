A road trip from Delhi to Mukteshwar is one of the most scenic drives you can take over a weekend. 

**Route Options:**
- Via Moradabad - Haldwani - Bhimtal - Bhowali
- Via Kaladhungi - Nainital (more scenic, but longer)

Make sure to stop by local dhabas and enjoy the scenic transition from the plains to the majestic Himalayas.
