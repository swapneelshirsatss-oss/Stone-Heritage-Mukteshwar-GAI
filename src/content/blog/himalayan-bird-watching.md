The South Gola Range is home to some of the most beautiful Himalayan birds. Whether you are an avid bird watcher or just a nature lover, the sights and sounds of the birds here will captivate you.

Keep your binoculars ready to spot Magpies, Woodpeckers, and even the majestic Himalayan Griffon!
